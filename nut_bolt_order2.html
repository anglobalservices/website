ISI FOR THE BOLTS, NUTS AND FASTERNERS 1363 (Part 2):2018


IS 1363 (Part 2):2018 /ISO 4018:2011 specifies the characteristics of hexagon head screws with thread size from M5 up to and including M64 of product grade C. Also, screws may be of different property classes as specified in IS 1363 (Part 2):2018/ ISO 4018:2011.

Hexagon Head Screw (Size ranges from M 5 to M 64) IS 1363 (Part 2):2018 /ISO 4018:2011 specifies the characteristics of hexagon head screws with thread size from M5 up to and including M64 of product grade C. Also, screws may be of different property classes as specified in IS 1363 (Part 2):2018/ ISO 4018:2011.


SCOPE:

This International Standard specifies the characteristics of hexagon head screws with threads from M5 up to and including M64, of product grade C. NOTE This type of product is the same as that covered by ISO 4016 with the exception of threading up to head. If, in special cases, specifications other than those listed in this International Standard are required, they should be selected from existing International Standards, for example ISO 724, ISO 888, ISO 898-1, ISO 965-1 and 1S0 4759-1.
