ISI FOR THE BOLTS, NUTS AND FASTERNERS 1363 (Part 1):2019


Hexagon Head Bolts, Screws and Nuts of product Grade C Hexagon Head Bolts (Size Range M 5 to M 64) IS 1363 (Part 1):2019, titled "Hexagon Head Bolts, Screws, and Nuts of Product Grade C (Size Range M 5 to M 64)" provides comprehensive guidelines for the manufacturing and testing of these fasteners in India. This standard ensures that the products meet specific quality requirements and conform to the specified dimensions, mechanical properties, and performance characteristics. Adhering to these standards not only ensures the safety and reliability of the fasteners but also facilitates interchangeability and compatibility in various applications.

The specified size range of M 5 to M 64 in IS 1363 (Part 1):2019 caters to a wide array of industrial and commercial applications. The designation "M" refers to metric threads, with the number indicating the major diameter of the fastener in millimetres. Hexagon head bolts, screws, and nuts in this size range find application in diverse sectors such as construction, automotive, machinery, and electronics. The use of standardized products ensures uniformity, ease of assembly, and maintenance, thus contributing to the overall efficiency and quality of the end product.

Product Grade C, as defined by IS 1363 (Part 1):2019, signifies a medium-strength category for hexagon head bolts, screws, and nuts. The standard specifies the mechanical properties, including the minimum tensile strength, yield strength, and elongation, which these fasteners must meet. This ensures that they possess sufficient strength and durability to withstand the intended loads and operating conditions. Additionally, the standard provides guidelines for the dimensional tolerance, surface finish, and other essential characteristics to guarantee the quality and reliability of the products.
