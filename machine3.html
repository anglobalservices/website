SPORTS SHOES SOLE MARKING MACHINE



In the production of sports shoes, on both the shoe sole and the upper materials marks can be applied with laser marking. The purpose of the marking is to achieve optical effects or to apply functional signs.

Production Capacity:

  • 200 Pairs/Hour

It can automatically adjust according to the shape of the bottom of all kinds of shoes, so that the front and rear are consistent with the inside. No need to pad any material to make the sole match with the shoe last, will not produce any error.

MAIN FEATURES

  1. At the bottom of this machine, many pieces of high strength and tenacious block rubber are used, and the pressure can be adjusted automatically.
  2. The front and rear pressure bar is an automatic cylinder, and the pressure can be adjusted and balanced automatically without adjusting the front and rear height of shoe last in operation.
  3. Save time, save labour and improve production efficiency.
  4. Use the manual adjustment to the inclining degree, more convenient and practical.
  5. Mark more correctly, it is good for the flow of thickening, gluing and attaching and so on.
  6. used for sport shoes, casual shoes.
