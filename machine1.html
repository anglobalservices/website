FOOTWEAR TESTING MACHINE

Footwear testing machines are designed to execute all types of tests relevant to footwear design and development and are ideal for testing sole adhesion, stitch tear, flexural properties, breaking tenacity of cotton fibres along with tear strength, peel strength and tensile strength of the shoe. To meet consumers special requirements, range of footwear test equipment comes in different force ranges and price ranges. We offer manual, motorized and digital test machines that all leave consumers with reliable, easy-to-read test results that conform to relevant standards within footwear testing.

Our digital test machines are supplied with a dedicated library of pre-programmed test setups and advanced analysis software displaying test results as graphs.

All of our test machines are modular. This means that by a simple change of grips you are able to perform different types of tests on varying materials regardless of shape and size using the same machine.
