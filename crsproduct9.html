Low - Voltage switchgear and controlgear: electromechanical contactors and motor - Starters-IS/IEC 60947-4-1 : 2012

Electromechanical Contactors


Electromechanical contactors and starters, including motor protective switching devices; electromechanical overload protection used in safety applications such as protecting a motor located in an explosive atmosphere.

The government of Indian and BIS is more concerned about the public health, to protect Indian consumers and maintain the proper quality of the products for the economic development as it will help in increasing the export.

Keeping this in consideration Government of Indian and BIS has made it compulsory for foreign as well as domestic manufacturers to register the low-voltage switchgear and controlgear: electromechanical contactor and motor-starters. And it must be manufactured as per the IS/IEC 60947: PART 4: SEC 1: 2012. Without BIS registration, no person can manufacture this product and sell it in the Indian market
