Electromechanical Control


Electromechanical devices are those devices that include both Electrical as well as mechanical processes, and their major function is that it provides an electrical signal to create the mechanical movement. An electrical device is used to control, signal, interlock, and so on switchgear and control gear.

A mechanical switching device is used to control the operation of switchgear or control gear, such as signaling, electrical interlocking, and so on.
