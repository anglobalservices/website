Draft QCO Notification for "Resin treated compressed wood laminates, Plywood and Wooden flush door shutters and Wood Based Boards" (Mandatory BIS Certification for Resin treated compressed wood laminates, Plywood and Wooden flush door shutters and Wood Based Boards)

Department for Promotion of Industry & Internal Trade has prepared a Draft Quality Control Orders in respect of Resin treated compressed wood laminates, Plywood and Wooden flush door shutters and Wood Based Boards in consultation with BIS in order to bring it under mandatory BIS certification keeping in view the human safety and for ensuring the optimum quality of product.
