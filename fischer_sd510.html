Goldscope SD 510



Goldscope SD 510 is a X-Ray Fluorescence Measuring gold testing instrument optimized for fast, cost-effective and non-destructive Analysis of jewellery, coins and precious metals for jewellery, retail store, banks and gold exchange.

Goldscope SD 510 can measure all the element from Aluminium to Uranium including the detection of PGM group elements such as Iridium, Ruthinuem, Osmium. It comes with an advanced SI - PIN detector for high-precision analysis of precious metals.
