ISI FOR THE BOLTS, NUTS AND FASTERNERS IS 1364 (Part 2):2018


IS 1364 (Part 2):2018/ISO 4017:2014 specifies the characteristics of Hexagon Head Screws with thread size from M 1.6 to up to and including M 64 of product grade A and B. Also, Screws may be of different property classes as specified in IS 1364 (Part 2):2018/ ISO 4017:2014


SCOPE:

This International Standard specifies the characteristics of hexagon head screws with threads from Ml,6 up to and including M64, of product grade A for threads Ml,6 to M24 and nominal lengths up to and including 10 d or 150 mm, whichever is shorter, and product grade B for threads over M24 or nominal lengths over 10 d or 150 mm, whichever is shorter. NOTE This type of product is the same as that covered by ISO 4014 with the exception of threading up to head and nominal length up to and including 200 mm as popular lengths. If, in special cases, specifications other than those listed in this International Standard are required, they should be selected from existing International Standards, for example ISO 724, ISO 888, ISO 898-1, ISO 965-1, ISO 3506-1 j ISO 4753 and ISO 4759-1.
