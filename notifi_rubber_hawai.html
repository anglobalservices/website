EXTENSION IN BIS CERTIFICATION (QCO ORDER) FOR SPORTS FOOTWEAR, RUBBER HAWAI CHAPPAL


RUBBER HAWAI CHAPPAL IS 10702:1992

To get BIS certification and produce a standard quality product, the manufacturer must ensure that their product must follow the specified Indian standard. Let's take a closer look at IS 10702:1992 for Rubber Hawai Chappal.

Rubber Hawai Chappals are covered under IS 10702:1992. BIS adopted this Indian standard after the draft finalized by the footwear sectional committee had been approved by the chemical division council.


SPORTS FOOTWEAR IS 15844:2010

Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) license will be mandatory for 24 footwear products from July 1, 2023.The Quality Control Order will now come into force from July 1, 2023. The standards for footwear products are developed in consultation with trade and industry bodies.

Sports footwear is covered under IS 15844:2010. The Bureau of Indian Standards adopted this Indian Standard after the Footwear Sectional Committee's final draft was approved by the Chemical Division Council.
