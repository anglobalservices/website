High Strength Structural Bolts IS 3757:1985


3757:1985 High Strength Structural Bolts High Strength Structural Bolts as per IS 3757: 1985 are classified as follows:
(i) Property Class: 8.8, 10.9 (ii) Size: M16, M20, M22, M24, M27, M30, M36
(iii) Finish - Dull Black (Property Class 8.8, 10.9) Hot-dip Galvanized (Property Class 10.9)


SCOPE:

This standard covers the requirements for hotdip galvanized hexagon head transmission tower bolts in the size range M12 to M24 for use in the construction of transmission towers, sub-stations and silmilar steel structures.
