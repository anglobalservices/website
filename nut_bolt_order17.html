High Strength Structural Nuts IS 6623:2004


SCOPE:

This standard covers the requirements of large series hexagon, high strength structural steel nuts of property classes 8 and 10 in the size range of M 12 to M 36 suitable for use in both friction-type and bearing-type of structural steel connections. Nuts to this standard when matched with the appropriate bolts have been designed to provide an assembly with a high level of assurance against failure by thread stripping on over tightening
